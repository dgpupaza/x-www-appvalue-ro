Event

We invite you to join us for a unique, hands-on experience that brings together the best of Red Hat® OpenShift® Virtualization and OpenShift® AI. This event is designed for VM administrators and infrastructure architects who want to explore how OpenShift can accelerate modernization and innovation.

During the OpenShift Virtualization workshop, you’ll work directly with the platform to understand its fundamentals while exploring VM importing, networking, storage, and more. This immersive session is crafted to give you the practical knowledge you need to reimagine your infrastructure strategy.

In the OpenShift AI lab, you’ll experiment with large language models, retrieval-augmented generation, and image detection while deploying an application that demonstrates how these technologies can streamline processes and unlock new business value. This hands-on session offers a practical introduction to the possibilities of AI/ML on OpenShift.

This is more than just a workshop—it’s an opportunity to expand your expertise, connect with experts, and discover new ways to transform your organization with Red Hat OpenShift.
